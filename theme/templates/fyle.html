Fyle

Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning based travel and employee expense management platform.

Fyle is an AI/ML powered travel and employee expense management and automation platform. It allows employees to automatically capture information from receipts, create reports and ensure business policy compliance. It reduces dependancies on manual data entry by offering a 1-click expense claim experience to employees through its email plugins and mobile app.

It provides a company’s finance department a real time visibility into employee related expenses. The result is a drastically lower turnaround time for reimbursements, accurate reports and audit-ready expense accounts.
