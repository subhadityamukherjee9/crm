PropertyShare is crowd-funding investment and trading platform in the real estate asset class. It allows multiple retail investors to start with small investment ticket sizes and purchase fractional ownership stakes in residential and commercial properties. Fractional owners receive rental income and capital appreciation gains in proportion to their respective ownership stakes.
