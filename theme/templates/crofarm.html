Crofarm

Technology enabled B2B marketplace for fresh foods.

Crofarm is a technology enabled B2B agricultural supply chain for fruits and vegetables, from farmers to businesses like retailers, wholesalers, restaurants etc. By leveraging its proprietary tools for supply chain management and logistics optimization, Crofarm is able to bring down the wastage of produce to 5%, as opposed to 50% wastage prevalent in traditional supply chains.
